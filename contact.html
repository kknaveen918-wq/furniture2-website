Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What are your shipping options?

We offer free white-glove delivery on all orders over ₹41,500 within India. Standard delivery typically takes 7-14 business days.

Do you offer custom furniture?

Yes! We work with skilled artisans to create custom pieces tailored to your specifications. Contact our design team for a consultation.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all standard items. Custom orders are final sale. Please see our full return policy for details.

Do you provide assembly services?

Yes, our white-glove delivery includes full assembly and placement of your furniture in your home.
