The LUXE Story

Founded in 1985 by master craftsman Antonio Rossi, LUXE began as a small workshop in the heart of Milan. What started as a passion for traditional woodworking techniques evolved into a mission to bring Italian elegance to homes around the world.

Today, LUXE collaborates with the finest designers and artisans to create furniture that bridges classic craftsmanship with contemporary aesthetics. Each piece is meticulously crafted using premium materials sourced from sustainable suppliers globally.

From our flagship showroom in New York to our online presence, we remain dedicated to the same principles that guided us from the beginning: exceptional quality, thoughtful design, and an unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction.
